Revelation of a World

Everything here feels strange — less like physical sensation, and more like receiving raw information. It begins with a general sense of space.

In an endless void where nothing truly exists, a world begins to emerge. At this moment, the first ideas take tangible shape — sparkling, as if they want to be seen. Some are already solid enough to traverse; others are still forming.

After the general overview, further impressions begin to surface. As your awareness sharpens, a clear intuition arises: focusing on certain keywords may reveal more than what is first perceived.


𖠁 Features

Ancient World
This is a world branded by time and events. As you explore its remnants, you will find many forgotten truths and challenges guarding them.

Tactical & Deckbuilding
To succeed in this world, you’ll need preparation — and the insight to adapt to its many challenges.

Role Playing Game
To wander this world, you’ll need to find your calling , grow stronger , and seek others to support you where you fall short.
