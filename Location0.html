Revelation of a World

Everything here feels strange — less like physical sensation, and more like receiving raw information. It begins with a general sense of space.

In an endless void where nothing truly exists, a world begins to emerge. At this moment, the first ideas take tangible shape — sparkling, as if they want to be seen. Some are already solid enough to traverse; others are still forming.

After the general overview, further impressions begin to surface — each aligned with a direction. As your awareness sharpens, a clear intuition arises: focusing on certain keywords may reveal more than what is first perceived.


Features

--- Center ---

Left : [ Sandbox Exploration ]

This idea speaks of an entire world to explore freely, with only choices and abilities setting the limits.

Middle : [ Tactical Planning ]

The idea that success depends mostly on learning what to prepare and how to adapt in the moment is found here.

Right : [ RPG Progression ]

Another idea forms: to follow the path of one of four unique Callings and choose which Attributes to grow. Attributes and Callings define who you become.

--- North ---

Left : [ Deckbuilding ]

Mix, match, and experiment with abilities like cards and discover new ones hidden throughout the world.

Right : [ Group Gameplay ]

A vision of traveling in groups of up to three, where companions are found either in this world or your own.

--- South ---

Left : [ Ancient World ]

The world is filled with the remnants of long-lost times, where every step brings new understanding .

Right : [ Diverse Challenges ]

Here, progress is not earned through combat alone. Fighting is just one of many approaches .
